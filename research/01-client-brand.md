# Phase 1: Client Brand Extraction — The Gold Standard by Hannah Goldy

## Brand Snapshot
- **Company:** The Gold Standard by Hannah Goldy
- **Primary Color:** #1C2B3A (Deep Navy) — primary background
- **Secondary Color:** #E8C96A (Light Gold) — accent on dark only
- **Accent Color:** #A8872A (Matte Gold) — gold on light backgrounds
- **White:** #F5F4F0 (Off-White) — text and clean backgrounds
- **Near Black:** #111827 — dark overlays
- **Navy Mid:** #2F4259 — secondary panels
- **Fonts:** Anton (display/headlines) / Bebas Neue (accent/subheads) / Inter (body/UI)
- **Tone:** Authoritative, athletic, confident, authentic
- **Core Message:** Professional-grade training system accessible to anyone — built by a UFC veteran who navigated fat loss with a hypothyroid condition and created the program she wished existed.

## Key Messaging & Value Proposition

### Unique Selling Points
- 4 NASM certifications
- BJJ black belt
- UFC veteran
- 12 years as a professional athlete
- Hypothyroid condition (disease that makes fat loss harder — proves she overcame the same struggle)
- "It took me 12 years — now you get all the knowledge at once"

### Dream Outcomes (for the customer)
1. Train your body to function better, at your optimal level
2. Feel good, look good
3. Feeling confident in all areas of your life
4. Be able to defend yourself ("don't f*** with me" energy)
5. Gain knowledge so you can do it on your own forever

### Fake Beliefs to Overcome
- "You've always been like this" (genetics excuse)
- "It's easy for you" (pro athlete privilege)
- "I can learn this myself" (took her 12 years)
- "I have to be a pro athlete to see results"
- "I have to train 6 hours a day"
- "I can't eat the foods I want"
- "I don't have the time"
- "I don't have the funds"
