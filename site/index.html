Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to be in great shape already? +

No. The Gold Standard is designed for anyone regardless of where they're starting. Whether you need to lose 50 pounds or you're looking to lean out and build muscle, the phased approach meets you where you are and progresses at your pace.

How much time do I need per day? +

The routines are built for real people with real schedules. Expect 45-60 minutes of training, 4-5 days a week. The nutrition side fits into your existing life without requiring meal-prep marathons.

Do I need a gym membership? +

Yes, access to a gym with basic equipment (barbells, dumbbells, cables, cardio machines) is recommended. You'll also need a calorie tracking app (free options available). The Getting Started module covers everything you need.

Can I do all three tracks? +

Absolutely. Many people start with the Fat Loss track and transition to Muscle Building or Fighter Conditioning, or vice versa. All three tracks share the same training principles, so you can switch based on your goals. You get lifetime access to all of them.

What if I have a medical condition like hypothyroidism? +

Hannah was diagnosed with hypothyroidism at age 10 and has been fighting it her entire life, not just as an adult. This program was literally born from that experience. That said, always talk to your doctor before starting any new fitness program.

How is this different from other online programs? +

Most online fitness programs are built by marketers. This one was built by a UFC veteran with a BJJ black belt and 12 years of professional experience. The Gold Standard is the system Hannah used on herself. It's not a theoretical framework designed to sell. It's not a cookie-cutter template. It's the real system she built through a decade of competing at the highest level.

Is there a refund policy? +

We stand behind this program. If you follow the system and don't see results, reach out to our team and we'll work with you. Details on the refund policy will be provided at checkout.

Do I need combat sports experience for the Fighter Conditioning track? +

No. The Fighter Conditioning track is designed to improve conditioning and athletic performance for anyone interested in training like a fighter. You don't need to step into a cage. That said, if you're a complete beginner, Hannah recommends starting with the Fat Loss or Muscle Building track first and progressing to Fighter Conditioning when you're ready.
