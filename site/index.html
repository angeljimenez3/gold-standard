Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to be in great shape already? +

No. The Gold Standard is designed for anyone regardless of where they're starting. Whether you need to lose 50 pounds or you're looking to lean out and build muscle, the phased approach meets you where you are and progresses at your pace.

How much time do I need per day? +

The routines are built for real people with real schedules. Expect 45-60 minutes of training, 4-5 days a week. The nutrition side fits into your existing life without requiring meal-prep marathons.

Do I need a gym membership? +

Yes, access to a gym with basic equipment (barbells, dumbbells, cables, cardio machines) is recommended. You'll also need a calorie tracking app (free options available). The Getting Started module covers everything you need.

Can I do both the Fat Loss and Muscle Building tracks? +

Absolutely. Many people start with the Fat Loss track and transition to Muscle Building, or vice versa. Both tracks share the same training principles, so you can switch based on your goals.

What if I have a medical condition like hypothyroidism? +

Hannah personally dealt with fat loss while managing hypothyroidism, a condition that makes weight management significantly harder. This program was born from that experience. That said, always talk to your doctor before starting any new fitness program.

How is this different from other online programs? +

Most online fitness programs are built by marketers. This one was built by a UFC veteran with 4 NASM certifications, a BJJ black belt, and 12 years of professional experience. The Gold Standard is the system Hannah used on herself. It's not a theoretical framework designed to sell.

Is there a refund policy? +

We stand behind this program. If you follow the system and don't see results, reach out to our team and we'll work with you. Details on the refund policy will be provided at checkout.

What's the difference between the course and group coaching? +

The course ($297) gives you the full program with lifetime access. Perfect for self-starters. Group coaching ($3,000) adds 8 weeks of live group calls with Hannah, advanced programming, and direct accountability. 1-on-1 coaching gives you private calls for your specific situation.
